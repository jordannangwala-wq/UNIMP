The Uganda National Mining Company (UNMC) was established under the Mining and Minerals Act 2022 to hold and manage the state's equity interest in Uganda's mineral sector. Under Section 4 of the MMA 2022, UNMC is entitled to a 15% free carry in all large-scale mining licenses, with the right to acquire up to 20% total at commercial rates.
