National Strategic Overview · Uganda
Uganda Mining Portfolio — 2025
Under the Mining and Minerals Act 2022, Uganda's national mining strategy is entering a transformational phase. The Makuutu REE project (LML00334) received Uganda's first large-scale mining licence in January 2024. Kilembe copper-cobalt mine was handed to UNMC in April 2025 after 43 years. UNMC holds a statutory 15% free-carry interest in all large-scale projects, with the right to acquire up to 20% additional equity at commercial rates. Mining now contributes 2.2% of GDP, up from 0.3% in 2010.
